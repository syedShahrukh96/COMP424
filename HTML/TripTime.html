Disney World, Orlando Florida

Disneyland, located in Anaheim, California, is a world-renowned theme park with iconic attractions and entertainment shows, famous for its eight themed lands and classic rides, appealing to families and Disney fans.

Universal Studios Florida

Universal Studios Florida is a theme park located in Universal Orlando Resort, Florida. It features rides and attractions based on popular movies and TV shows, including Harry Potter, Transformers, and The Simpsons.

Niagara Falls, New York

Niagara Falls is a city in New York, located on the Niagara River and home to the famous Niagara Falls State Park. It is a popular tourist destination known for its natural beauty and attractions.

San Diego, California

San Diego is a coastal city located in Southern California, USA. Known for its beautiful beaches, mild climate, and cultural attractions, it is a popular destination for tourists and residents alike.

Hershey Park, Pennsylvania

Hersheypark is a theme park in Hershey, Pennsylvania. It features roller coasters, water rides, and family-friendly attractions. The park is owned by The Hershey Company and has a chocolate theme throughout.

LEGOLAND, California

LEGOLAND California is a theme park in Carlsbad, California, featuring over 60 rides and attractions designed for families with children ages 2 to 12. The park is themed around LEGO toys and features LEGO sculptures throughout.

Washington, D.C.

Washington, D.C. is the capital city of the United States, located on the east coast of the country. It is home to many famous landmarks and government buildings, including the White House and the National Mall.

Chicago, Illinois

Chicago is a major city in the midwestern United States, located on the shore of Lake Michigan. It is known for its architecture, museums, music, and sports teams, including the Chicago Cubs and Chicago Bulls.
